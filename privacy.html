Pixora – Privacy Policy

Your data, your trust — our responsibility.

1. Introduction

Pixora prioritizes user privacy and strives to keep all data secure and protected in accordance with standard practices and the security requirements defined in our Software Requirements Specification (SRS).

2. Data We Collect

  • Full Name and Email
  • Login Credentials (encrypted)
  • Booking Details
  • Payment Details (via third-party secure gateways)
  • Reviews and Feedback

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Verify your identity and login (REQ-1)
  • Store booking details securely (REQ-4)
  • Send booking confirmations & email notifications
  • Process payments via authorized gateways

4. Data Protection & Security

In compliance with security requirements:

  • All sensitive data is stored in encrypted form.
  • User sessions are safely terminated after logout.
  • Failed transactions are automatically reverted.
  • Protection against SQL Injection, XSS & CSRF attacks.
  • Daily data backups are maintained for safety.

5. Third-Party Services

Payments are handled only via authorized third-party gateways. Pixora does not store credit card information directly on its servers.

6. User Rights

  • Access your data anytime.
  • Request account deletion.
  • Edit your personal information.
  • Submit queries via our FAQ section.

7. Misuse & Fraud Policy

Any attempt at fraudulent activity may lead to account suspension and legal action. Admins hold the right to block vendors/users in case of suspicious behavior.
