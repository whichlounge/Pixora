Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your perfect photography session.

Booking FAQs

How do I book a photographer? +
You can easily book a photographer by browsing available professionals, selecting your preferred date and time, and confirming the booking with a secure payment.
Can I reschedule my booking? +
Yes! You can reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before your scheduled session without any additional charges.
Do I need to pay upfront? +
A small deposit is required to secure your slot. The remaining balance can be paid on the day of your session.

Photo Delivery FAQs

How long does it take to receive my photos? +
Most photographers deliver edited photos within 5–7 business days. Delivery time may vary depending on the package and editing requirements.
Will I receive raw photos? +
Raw images are not typically included but can be requested directly from your photographer for an additional fee.
How are photos delivered? +
Photos are delivered digitally via a private gallery link that you can download, share, or order prints from directly.
